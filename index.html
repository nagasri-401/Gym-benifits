Training Process

  1. Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio and stretching.
  2. Main Workout: Strength or cardio training as per your plan.
  3. Cool Down: 5-10 minutes of stretching to relax muscles.
  4. Nutrition: Balanced meal after workout.

Benefits of Gym Training

  • Improves strength and endurance
  • Boosts mental health and mood
  • Helps in weight management
  • Improves flexibility and posture
  • Enhances overall fitness
